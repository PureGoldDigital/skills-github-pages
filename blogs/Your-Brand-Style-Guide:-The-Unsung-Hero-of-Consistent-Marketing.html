What is a Brand Style Guide?

A brand style guide (also called a brand book or brand bible) is a comprehensive document that outlines the visual and verbal elements of your brand. It keeps your message and image consistent across every channel and platform.

Core Components

  • Logo usage: Guidelines on placement, sizing, and color variations
  • Color palette: Primary and secondary colors with hex/RGB codes
  • Typography: Font families, sizes, and formatting rules
  • Imagery: Photo and illustration styles that support your brand feel
  • Voice & tone: Examples of how your brand sounds in different contexts

Without a brand guide, you risk inconsistency ‚Äî which erodes trust, confuses your audience, and dilutes brand equity. With one, you empower every department (and every vendor) to speak and design with one clear voice.
